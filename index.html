About us

It Started in 2012 with just 5 childrean. With persistent efforts and hardwork from the team, they are able to provide not only shelter, food and education to a number of under-privileged childrean, tribal kids, child labours but also carved out a Home and reason for living for them. It is a pleasure to know the fact that these blossming buds are provided with enough love and care to make them self sufficient emotionally, physically and economically . In order to meet the demand of the place, the team go door-to-door and collect the donations.We are trying to carve out a discipliend lifestyle in childrean. Kindly visit the Ashrama to see the brightness in childrean.

Our Vision

A socity where old aged live in comfort & dignity in the last phase of life, childrean get love, care and guidance which makes each of them a good human being in future, empower distresses women and work to build a healthy, hunger free world.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to make our little kids to stand on their own and be good citizens to our nation.
